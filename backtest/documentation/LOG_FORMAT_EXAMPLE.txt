================================================================================
KEY INSIGHTS FROM LOGS
================================================================================

What You'll Learn:

1. STOP LOSS & TAKE PROFIT
   - Optimal SL/TP ratios for each symbol type
   - Forex: typically 15-30 pips SL, 30-60 pips TP
   - JPY: same pip counts but different values
   - Metals: 300-1000 pips (much wider)

2. BREAKEVEN & TRAILING STOPS
   - When to move to breakeven (usually 15-20 pips for forex)
   - When to activate trailing stop (usually 15-20 pips)
   - Trailing distance (10-15 pips for forex)

3. BEST TRADING HOURS
   - Entry windows: typically 8-10am to 4-6pm
   - Exit hours: typically 6-8pm
   - Avoids overnight risk and low-liquidity periods

4. BEST TRADING DAYS
   - Usually Tuesday-Friday are best
   - Monday: delayed entry (wait until 10-12am)
   - Friday: early exit (close by 4-5pm)
   - Avoids weekend gaps and Monday volatility

5. VALIDATION PROVES ROBUSTNESS
   - Parameters work across 3 years (not curve-fit)
   - Still profitable in recent 3 months
   - Still profitable in last 1 month
   - Only approved if ALL periods are profitable
