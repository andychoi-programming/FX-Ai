  → Adds 35 pip SL for wider stop strategies

TAKE PROFIT (TP):
  2-Hour: [60, 75, 90, 120] - 4 levels
  24-Hour: [60, 75, 90, 105, 120] - 5 levels
  → Adds 105 pip TP (tests 1:3 ratio with 35 pip SL)

BREAKEVEN:
  2-Hour: [15, 25] - 2 levels (tight vs loose)
  24-Hour: [15, 20, 25] - 3 levels (tight/medium/loose)
  → Adds medium 20 pip option

FRIDAY EXIT:
  2-Hour: [20] - 1 option (8 PM MT5 time)
  24-Hour: [19, 20, 21] - 3 options (7-9 PM MT5)
  → Tests different weekend risk management approaches

BEST DAYS:
  2-Hour: Tue-Fri only (skip Monday)
  24-Hour: Tue-Fri OR Mon-Thu (2 patterns)
  → Quantifies Monday volatility impact

METALS SL/TP:
  2-Hour: [300-1000] / [1200-3000]
  24-Hour: [300-1200] / [1200-3600]
  → Wider range for large metal movements
